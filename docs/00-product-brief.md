# RuangRapi Product Brief

## Summary

RuangRapi is a SaaS for Indonesian rental property owners who manage kos, kontrakan, apartments, or small rental portfolios.

The product helps owners and admins manage rental operations, monthly billing, payment tracking, tenant records, receipts, reminders, utility readings, maintenance tickets, and simple reporting.

## Problem

Many small rental owners in Indonesia manage rental operations using WhatsApp, notebooks, spreadsheets, and memory.

Common problems:

- Forgetting who has paid
- Manual monthly billing
- Hard-to-track tenant lease periods
- No consistent receipt format
- Maintenance requests scattered across chat
- Utility readings tracked manually
- No simple monthly overview
- Difficulty managing multiple properties or units

## Target Customer

Primary customer:

- Small to medium rental property owner in Indonesia

Examples:

- Kos owner with 10 to 50 rooms
- Kontrakan owner with several units
- Apartment owner renting several units
- Family business managing rental properties
- Admin managing rent collection for an owner

## Product Type

RuangRapi is an operations SaaS.

It is not:

- A property marketplace
- A tenant acquisition platform
- A full accounting system
- A large enterprise property management system

## MVP Value Proposition

RuangRapi helps rental owners know:

- Which units are occupied
- Which tenants are active
- Which monthly bills are unpaid
- Which payments have been received
- Which receipts were issued
- Which maintenance tickets are open
- How much rent should be collected this month
- How much rent has actually been collected

## MVP Success Criteria

The MVP is successful if a small property owner can use it to manage one month of rental operations without relying on a spreadsheet as the primary source of truth.
