# Target Customers

## Primary users

RuangRapi targets Indonesian rental owners and managers who operate:

- kos
- kontrakan
- apartment rental units
- small multi-unit properties
- ruko or small commercial rentals
- mixed small rental portfolios

## Typical user profile

The ideal early user may:

- manage between 3 and 50 rental units
- communicate with tenants through WhatsApp
- currently track rent manually or with spreadsheets
- need reminders for monthly payments
- want better records without complex software
- prefer simple workflows over advanced enterprise features

## User pain points

- forgetting who has paid
- scattered tenant contact information
- unclear rent due dates
- manual receipt handling
- repeated WhatsApp follow-ups
- no clear view of vacant or occupied units
- hard-to-track maintenance issues
- messy historical records

## Early product promise

RuangRapi should help owners answer:

```txt
Who rents which unit?
What is due this month?
Who has paid?
Who still needs a reminder?
What units or properties need attention?
```
