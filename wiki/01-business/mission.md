# Mission

Help Indonesian rental owners manage daily rental operations with less confusion, fewer missed payments, and clearer records.

## Mission statement

RuangRapi exists to make rental operations tidy, traceable, and manageable for small landlords and property operators.

## What success looks like

A user should be able to:

- register properties and units
- store tenant records
- connect tenants to units through leases
- generate or track monthly rent obligations
- record payments
- issue receipts
- follow up through reminders
- track maintenance issues
- see simple operational reports

## What RuangRapi is not

RuangRapi is not primarily:

- a real estate marketplace
- a property listing portal
- a tenant social app
- a full accounting system
- an enterprise property management platform

It is a focused rental operations tool.
