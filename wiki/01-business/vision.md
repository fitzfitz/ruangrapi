# Vision

Build a simple, trusted rental operations platform for Indonesian rental owners.

RuangRapi should help small and growing rental owners move from scattered notebooks, chat messages, spreadsheets, and memory-based tracking into one organized operating system.

## Long-term vision

RuangRapi becomes the practical command center for rental operations in Indonesia:

- know what properties and units exist
- know who rents what
- know what is due
- know who has paid
- know what needs follow-up
- know what needs repair
- know how the rental business is performing

## Product direction

The product should stay operational, not decorative.

A feature is valuable when it helps an owner answer one of these questions:

- What do I own or manage?
- Who is renting from me?
- What agreement is active?
- What money is due?
- Who has paid?
- What needs reminding?
- What needs fixing?
- What does the business look like this month?
