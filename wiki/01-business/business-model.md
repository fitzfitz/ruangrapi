# Business Model

## Initial business model hypothesis

RuangRapi can start as a subscription SaaS for rental owners and small property managers.

Possible pricing dimensions:

- number of properties
- number of units
- number of active tenants or leases
- reminder volume
- advanced reporting
- team/user seats

## Possible plans

### Starter

For small landlords.

Potential limits:

- small number of properties
- small number of units
- basic tenant, lease, billing, and payment tracking

### Growth

For owners with larger portfolios.

Potential features:

- higher unit limits
- WhatsApp reminder support
- receipts
- maintenance tracking
- reports

### Pro / Operator

For professional managers.

Potential features:

- multi-user roles
- advanced reports
- exports
- audit trails
- automation

## Monetization should wait for product proof

The MVP should focus on validating operational value:

- Does the product reduce admin work?
- Does it make payment tracking easier?
- Do owners return monthly?
- Do owners trust the records?
- Do reminders create measurable value?
