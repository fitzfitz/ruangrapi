# Post-MVP Roadmap

Post-MVP work should deepen operational value after the core rental loop is proven.

## Possible post-MVP areas

### Lifecycle management

- archive/inactive properties
- archive/inactive tenants
- unit occupancy lifecycle
- lease renewal
- lease cancellation

### Advanced billing

- recurring invoice generation
- late fees
- prorated rent
- discounts
- deposits
- utility charge automation

### WhatsApp automation

- scheduled reminders
- bulk reminders
- payment confirmation messages
- message templates
- reminder logs

### Documents and files

- tenant ID document storage
- lease PDFs
- payment proof uploads
- property/unit images
- maintenance photos

### Reporting

- cashflow reports
- payment aging
- occupancy reports
- property performance
- Excel/PDF exports

### Team features

- owner/admin/staff roles
- permissions
- audit logs
- assigned staff workflows

### Tenant portal

- tenant invoice view
- tenant payment proof upload
- tenant maintenance requests
